2 VML shapes and css triangles joined, and relative v:rect on top

It's pretty trciky.. We join two html triangles (css borders) and two VML shapes, and we apply a maximum height of the desired height, so that the text box overlaps and comes above the two triangles. The result is text positioned in absolute above an inclined bar. We wrap the text box in a div in relative position, with also an opacity of 0.999, so that the text remains on top. We could use a negative margin on the text block, but it does not pass on gmail and full of webmail. This maximum height solution is therefore perfect, because you can really adjust the placement of the text without adding a negative margin.
